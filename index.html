Game Boy

Play and enjoy some of your most loved childhood Games

Tenzies game

A javascript simulation of the classic Tenzies game. Every player here gets 10 dice, and the objectve of the game is to roll the dice as fast as possible, until all dice are on the same number and yells "TENZI!" is the winner.

Rock paper scissors

The Rock-paper-scissors is a hand game usually played by two people where players are allowed to pick one of three different shapes, Rock paper or scissors.

Black Jack

The Blackjack or twenty-one, is a Card game whose objective is to be dealt cards having a higher count than those of the dealer, but not exceeding 21. The Aces count as 1 or 11, and face cards count as 10.

Tic tac toe

Tic tac toe Tic-tac-toe is a game in which two players take turns in drawing either an ' O' or an ' X' in one square of a grid consisting of nine squares. The first player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row is the winner.
